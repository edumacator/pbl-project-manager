# Product Vision

This product is a lightweight project orchestration system for high school project-based learning (PBL). It blends project-management structure with student scaffolds, teacher coaching visibility, structured reflection, and peer critique.

## What this product is
- A practical classroom tool for running and tracking PBL work.
- A structure that helps students plan, execute, reflect, and collaborate.
- A visibility layer that helps teachers coach teams and individuals.

## What this product is not
- Not a generic enterprise project management suite.
- Not a surveillance tool.
- Not a full LMS replacement.
- Not a bloated all-in-one platform.

## Primary users
- **Teachers**: project designers, facilitators, coaches, and administrators.
- **Students**: contributors, reflective learners, and peer collaborators.

## Priority statement
Student usability and educational value come first; feature breadth comes second.
