## Seed Decisions

### Focus on Cognitive Scaffolding
- **Date**: 2026-03-15
- **Status**: accepted
- **Context**: Initial product direction.
- **Decision**: Prioritize tools that help students think (checkpoints, reflections) over tools that simply track time.
- **Reasoning**: The core educational value is support for meta-cognition, not just PM logistics.

### Teacher Visibility as Coaching Support
- **Date**: 2026-03-15
- **Status**: accepted
- **Context**: Privacy and pedagogical approach.
- **Decision**: Teacher dashboards should focus on showing *where* teams are stuck, not recording every keystroke.
- **Reasoning**: Avoid turning the tool into a surveillance platform; keep the focus on productive intervention.

### Simplicity Over Enterprise Complexity
- **Date**: 2026-03-15
- **Status**: accepted
- **Context**: User demographic (High School).
- **Decision**: Reject feature requests for advanced enterprise Gantt charts or complex resource allocation.
- **Reasoning**: Keep the cognitive load low for students; focus on "the next right step."
